# Evolith: North Star Vision

**What is Evolith?**
A programmable operating system for exploring, verifying, and evolving human knowledge.

**Why should it exist?**
Because knowledge is currently trapped in static documents, isolated databases, and linear search engines. Humanity lacks a unified, spatial, and temporal interface to understand how ideas connect, evolve, and influence the future. Evolith exists to make the evolution of knowledge visible, verifiable, and programmable.

**What problems will it never compromise on?**
1. **Provenance & Verification:** Every node and edge must have a traceable source. No hallucinated connections.
2. **Spatial & Temporal Continuity:** Knowledge exists in time and space. The interface must respect physics and memory (e.g., no jumping nodes during time travel).
3. **Extensibility (Platform-First):** Evolith is not a monolith. It is an ecosystem built on open schemas, "Knowledge Packs," and plugins. It must remain extensible by the community.

**What will make it different 10 years from now?**
In 10 years, Evolith will not be seen as a "search engine" or an "AI research tool." It will be the underlying protocol and visual interface for how the world's universities, labs, and creators publish and interact with structured knowledge. It will be the "GitHub of Human Knowledge."
